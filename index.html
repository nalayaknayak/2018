FAQ

When is saMMsCRIthi'18?

The cultural, academic and sports festival will be held over 3 days starting September 21 to September 23.

Who can participate?

Entry is open to everyone to  concerts,cultural and literary events. Only medical students are allowed to take part in sports events ( except 6 a side cricket) and academic events.

How do I register?

Contact usby filling the form below.

What about accomodation?

Rooms will be provided at Rs 200 per night. These will be double occupancy rooms.
